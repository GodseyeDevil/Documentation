Max-A3

On October 26th, 2024, I initiated the development of the Max-A3 plotter. This A3-sized machine will be the prototype for a series of 28 plotters destined for the Center for Early Innovation Labs under SSK. In collaboration with Fablab Kerala and,Kerala Startup Mission , we aim to provide students with an educational STEM kit. The Max-A3, named after its maximum size, is designed to be both a functional plotter and a learning tool. Students can disassemble and reassemble the machine to gain practical experience in CNC and machine operation.

We reviewed multiple concept designs for the machine. These included standard designs, a design with the back panel facing away from the user, and a design with only one side exposed. After considering three primary concepts, we decided to proceed with the third option.
